BDR Massacre: Commission may ask for Hasina extradition or question her in India

Published on :6 Jan 2025

The National Independent Investigation Commission, formed to re-investigate the 2009 BDR (Bangladesh Rifles, now Border Guard Bangladesh) massacre, will try to speak with Sheikh Hasina by visiting India.
Maj Gen (retd) ALM Fazlur Rahman, the commission chief and former director general of BGB, disclosed the developments following a views exchange meeting with the BDR massacre martyr families at the Retired Armed Forces Officers Welfare Association (RAOWA) club.
"Those with suspected involvement, especially Sheik Hasina, are now staying in India.
